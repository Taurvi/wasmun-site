Washington State Model United Nations (WASMUN) is Washington State's largest high school Model United Nations conference. Held annually in the Seattle area, high school students participate in a two-day simulation of international organizations such as the United Nations. WASMUN is an officially registered nonprofit with the State of Washington, and is entirely run by a volunteer staff of college students and alumni in the greater Seattle area.

OUR MISSION

Washington State Model United Nations (WASMUN) aspires to develop students into informed global citizens by facilitating an interactive and academic simulation where students debate, discuss, and analyze complex international issues.
