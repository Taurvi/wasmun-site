Conference Information

WASMUN 2016 is going to be hosted from March 5th to March 6th at the University of Washington.

Registration Information

Priority registration will open on January 1, 2016. The last day to register will be February 20, 2016.

Committees

The following committees are going to be simulated at WASMUN 2016:

  • International Court of Justice (ICJ)
  • Human Rights Council (HRC)
  • Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)
  • UN Environmental Program (UNEP)
  • UN Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC)
  • General Assembly 4 (GA4)
