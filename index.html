WASMUN

Washington State Model United Nations

The Washington State Model United Nations (WASMUN) is Washington State's largest High School Model United Nations conference. Held annually in the Seattle area, high school students from throughout the Pacific Northwest participate in a weekend-long simulation of the United Nations. WASMUN is an officially registered nonprofit with the State of Washington, and is entirely run by a volunteer staff of students and alumni in the greater Seattle area.

WASMUN is hosted by Model United Nations at the University of Washington (UW-MUN).
For more information visit www.uw-mun.org.

Our Mission

Washington State Model United Nations (WASMUN) develops students into informed global citizens by facilitating an interactive simulation where students debate, discuss, and analyze complex international issues.
